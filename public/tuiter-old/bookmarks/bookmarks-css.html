Tesla Cybertruck production teased in $227M+ order for parts

It was recently reported that Tesla placed a large order for Cybertruck parts. Now, according to news published by the South Korean website Maekyung.com, the supplier has confirmed the order. As the story goes, the US EV maker placed an order for A, B, and C Pillar parts for the Tesla Cybertruck. The total order was worth some 300 billion won ($227 million), with the parts coming from supplier Seoyeon E-Hwa. According to Teslarati, Seoyeon E-Hwa is a division of the Korean Composite Stock Price Indexes (KOPSI), which works to track the Korean Stock Exchange. However, interestingly, the company also produces interior parts for vehicles, along with various pillars.
